Hi!

I am a 5th year PhD candidate in the Departement of Physics and Astronomy at UCLA. My research centers on the use of strong gravitational lensing by galaxies as a probe of the nature and origins of dark matter.

I've included a link to where you can watch a couple animations I made of strong gravitational lensing, which in my opinion is one of the most spectacular and in-your-face examples of mind-bending weirdness of our universe. Some of the content there was made with the software pyHalo, a python package I wrote to quickly generate realizations of dark matter halos in lens galaxies and along the line of sight, and lens them. If you're interested in using this software for your lensing projects, feel free to contact me.
